Fair questions, straight answers

Should I just use a physician loan program?

If you're employed W-2 — quite possibly, and we'll route you there when it wins. Private-practice, 1099, and K-1 surgeons usually need specialist documentation instead.

Can locum tenens surgeons qualify?

Yes — 1099 programs read facility contracts at roughly 90% of gross with 12–24 months of history; multiple facilities are normal.

My practice write-offs make my taxable income look small.

Deposit-based and P&L programs qualify you on the practice's real cash flow — your tax strategy stays intact.

Can a HELOC fund a surgical-group or ASC buy-in?

Yes — it's a common physician use: home-equity pricing beats liquidating investments, and the first mortgage stays untouched.
